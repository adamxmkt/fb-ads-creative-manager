## Common Operations

### Search Creatives
Use the search box in the Creative Library to quickly find creatives

### Filter by Status
- **draft** - Draft
- **running** - Running
- **paused** - Paused
- **ended** - Ended

### Update Creative Status
In the Creative Library, expand a creative and use the dropdown to update its status

### Preview Materials
In the Creative Library, expand a creative to preview uploaded images and videos
